Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Social via Email is a free, open-source, client-side web application.

Information We Collect

Social via Email is purely client-side (React app running in your browser). It does not have a server and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us (the developer).

  • We do not store your email address, contacts, chats, or conversations.
  • All data remains in your own email account (Gmail).
  • The app uses short-lived OAuth 2.0 access tokens from Google. These tokens are held only in browser memory and expire after 1 hour (or when you sign out/refresh).
  • No cookies, no localStorage, no persistent storage is used for user data.

Google OAuth & Data Access

When you sign in with Gmail:

  • The app requests limited OAuth scopes to read/send emails only as needed for the social features (contacts, chats, conversations).
  • Your data is processed entirely in your browser.
  • We (the developer) never see or receive your Google data.

The use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Hosting & Third Parties

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard web server logs (IP address, browser, etc.) as any web host does. See GitHub's privacy statement.

Your Rights & Data Ownership

You own everything: your email ID, your data, and even the app itself (MIT license — feel free to fork it).
